Flourless Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookie

Ingredients
  • 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 flax egg (1tbsp flax+ 3 tbsp water)
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup chocolate morsels (I use Enjoy Life brand)
Preparation
  1. 1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. 2. Stir together peanut butter and next 4 ingredients in a medium bowl until well blended
  3. 3. stir in chocolate morsels
  4. 4. Drop dough by rounded spoonfuls 2 inches apart onto parchment paper-lined baking sheets
  5. 5. Bake at 350° for 12 to 14 minutes or until puffed and lightly browned
  6. 6. Cool on baking sheets on a wire rack 5 minutes.
  7. 7. Transfer to wire rack, and let cool 15 minutes.

Mushroom Bacon

Ingredients
  • 8 oz of portobello mushroom caps thinly sliced
  • 3 Tbspn grapeseed oil
  • salt and ground black pepper
  • 1 Tbspn tamari • 1 Tbspn maple syrup
  • 1 tsp liquid smoke
  • 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp light brown sugar
Preparation
  1. preheat oven to 350 Farenheit
  2. line baking sheet with parchment paper and spray with cooking oil
  3. arrange mushrooms in a single layer and brush them with oil
  4. season lightly with salt and pepper
  5. bake for 15 min
  6. turn and return to oven for 10 more mins
  7. shallow bowl combine all other ingredients
  8. then take hot mushrooms and place in bowl with marinade and coat well
  9. return mushrooms to the oven for another 5 mins 10. enjoy.
